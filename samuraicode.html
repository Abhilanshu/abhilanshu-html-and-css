The ancient dragon, a majestic and timeless being, embodies the wisdom and power of the ages. Its colossal wings, weathered by centuries of flight, cast shadows over vast landscapes as it soars through the skies. Scales, glistening like molten gold or deep obsidian, tell stories of battles fought and mysteries uncovered.

The **samurai**, Japan’s elite warrior class, upheld loyalty, honor, and discipline through the **Bushidō** code. Masters of the **katana**, they served feudal lords, defending their domains with unmatched skill. Their armor, crafted with precision, symbolized strength. Many committed **seppuku** to preserve dignity in defeat. Though samurai rule ended with the **Meiji Restoration**, their legacy lives on in Japanese culture, martial arts, and philosophy, inspiring generations worldwide.
